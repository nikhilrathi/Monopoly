This is a simple Monopoly Game where 2 players compete to reach the end point.

Both players start at the first Square - Reunion Tower and their objective is to reach the SMU Square with more money than the other.

They take turns and spin a number to move.

When a player arrives at a Prize Square. he/she gets $100
When a player arrives at a Penalty Square, he/she losses $200
And When a player arrives at the Last Square, he/she gets $300 

The game ends when any one of the players reaches the Last Square.
The winner among the two is the player with more money.

Please find pictures of the game board and the spinner attached.
The Squares are read in from a file - squaresList.txt (also attached)
